FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What Can I donate?

Money, books or stationery. We do not need donation of textbooks as our teachers are prepared with their own learning material.

What subjects are taught?

We offer the following classes: Mathematics, English, Bahasa Indonesia, Reading & Writing, Science, and Character-building.

Where can I find The Little House?

  • The Little House 1: Tanah Merah
  • The Little House 2: Cakung
  • The Little House 3: Rusun Albo

What time does the school open?

We are open on Monday to Friday at 3:30pm until 5:00pm WIB

What grades are taught?

Students of grade 1 to 6 are welcome in every school. The little house 3 also offers classes to SMP&SMA students
